## Calculation basis

Duty is calculated per litre of pure alcohol in the product, based on alcohol by volume (ABV) bands. The same litre-of-pure-alcohol basis applies across all product types since the August 2023 duty reform.

## Standard rates by product type (£ per litre of pure alcohol)

**Beer:**
- 0–1.2% ABV: £0.00
- 1.3–3.4% ABV: £9.96
- 3.5–8.4% ABV: £22.58
- 8.5–22% ABV: £30.62
- Above 22% ABV: £33.99

## Draught Relief

Lower rates apply to draught products (packaged in containers of 20+ litres designed to connect to dispensing systems), ranging from £0 to £19.45 per litre of pure alcohol depending on product type and ABV band.

## Small Producer Relief

Reduced rates are available to small producers (replacing the old Small Brewers Relief); the relief tapers by annual production volume and applies to products below 8.5% ABV. See the gov.uk Small Producer Relief guidance for the rate calculator.
