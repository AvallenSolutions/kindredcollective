# Supplier Intelligence

**What this is:** the routing hub for supplier knowledge mined from the WhatsApp archives ([[whatsapp-kindred-spirits]], [[whatsapp-kc-general]]). This is the community-evidence layer behind the [[supplier-marketplace]] — who members actually use, rate and warn about. Sentiments are date-stamped community opinion, not verified fact; suppliers change.

## Strongest cross-year consensus (multiple independent endorsements, multiple years)

- **[[albatrans]]** — freight forwarding (EU, Mexico, worldwide); the most consistently endorsed logistics partner in the archive (2021–2026).
- **[[law-distribution]]** — bonded 3PL rated best-value for years, until its 2025 small-brand exit (page has both halves of the story).
- **Label Apeel** — the most-recommended label printer (2021–2026): fast, high quality, "half the price of Berkshire", handled duty-stamp label integration. Reflex Label Plus a close second (repeated last-minute saves, incl. 10pm label deliveries).
- **Halo Business Support** — outsourced back-office/order management; "a lifeline for small businesses" (2022–2026).
- **SupplyChain21** — bonded UK pallet movement and customs; repeatedly endorsed 2021–2026 (one bad EU–UK experience 2025).
- **Amsterdam Warehouse Company** — the default EU bonded warehouse recommendation (2022–2026).
- **Bowimi** — drinks-specific field-sales CRM; consistently praised (with price-rise grumbles), 2022–2026.
- **William Fox Syrups** — bulk flavour syrups with a Kindred discount (2023 onward).
- **NFU Mutual** — distillery insurance: "not cheapest but will actually pay out" (2022–2026).
- **SeedLegals** — fundraising/cap-table admin; endorsed overall, with recurring advice to self-file EIS certificates rather than pay their £500+ fee.
- **FSB (Federation of Small Businesses)** — ~£49/yr for legal templates, advice line and insurance discounts; repeatedly called the best small-brand value (2022–2026).
- **Portman Group** — free compliance advice for producers of any size, 48-hour turnaround; increasingly proactive (2025 audit of 500 products).

## Repeated warnings (factual, as reported by members)

- **[[enotria]]** — chronic slow payment reported by many brands, 2023–2025 (acquired by Majestic 2025).
- **Codestorm** — cheap but service complaints across 2021–2024 ("Codeshitstorm"); still used "for the price".
- **Bruni / Bruni Erben** — glass: quality fine, but lead-time and price-hike complaints 2021–2023 ("would not recommend to a friend").
- **Huboo** — fulfilment: "don't do it" (2022, 2024).
- **Walkers Logistics** — "a disaster", avoid (2024, 2026).
- **Foodies Festivals** — inflated footfall promises, competing bars; "rip off" consensus (2022, 2024–25).
- **Connectd / UK Angel Investment Network** — investor-matching: consensus "avoid"/poor ROI (2025).
- **Tide** — business banking: froze an account mid-raise; widely panned (2024). Starling most trusted; Revolut not FSCS-protected.
- **Delli** — consignment-model switch tanked members' sales; poor customer service (2026).
- **Ace Packaging** — members reported undelivered paid orders and a £20k loss; pursued via small claims (2023–24). See also [[scam-warnings]].

## How to use this hub

Query workflow: check this page for the consensus, then follow the category concept page for prices, contacts' companies, MOQs and dates. When new WhatsApp exports are ingested, update sentiment here **only** when multiple independent reports agree; single experiences stay on the category pages.
