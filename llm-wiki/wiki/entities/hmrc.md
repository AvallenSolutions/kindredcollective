# HMRC

**What this is:** His Majesty's Revenue and Customs, the UK tax authority. For [[kindred-collective]] members, HMRC is the regulator behind [[alcohol-duty]] — it publishes the duty rates, administers Draught Relief and Small Producer Relief, and approves alcohol producers.

Current rates guidance is captured in [[gov-uk-alcohol-duty-rates]] (as of Feb 2026). Rates change at fiscal events (Budgets), so HMRC guidance pages should be re-captured after each one.
