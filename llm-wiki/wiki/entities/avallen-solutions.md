# Avallen Solutions

**What this is:** a sustainability consultancy for drinks brands (avallen.solutions), spun out in July 2024 from the team behind Avallen Calvados. It operates and rebuilt the [[kindred-collective]] platform, and authored the January 2026 website rebuild PRD ([[prd-website-rebuild]]).

## Role in the community (from the archives)

- **Legislative explainer-in-residence**: published the plain-English guides the group relied on for [[extended-producer-responsibility]], [[deposit-return-scheme]], the CMA greenwashing crackdown (new direct-fining powers from April 2025), climate impact on drinks commodities, and the 2025 B Corp standards overhaul.
- **B Corp support**: runs workshops and supports member brands through certification/recertification under the new standards; a B Corp sub-group split off the main WhatsApp (2025).
- **Platform builder**: volunteered the full kindredcollective.co.uk rebuild, launched ~30 March 2026 — built with Claude Code, alongside a new venture rebranding as **Alkatera** (2026).

Heritage note: Avallen Calvados was itself a long-standing community member — B Corp certified (2022), paper-bottle pioneer (Frugalpac, 2022 — see [[glass-supply]]), Best Tasting Spirit at the 2024 Kindred Awards, and the trademark-defence case study in the archive.
