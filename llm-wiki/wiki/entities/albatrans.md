# Albatrans

**What this is:** an international freight forwarder (London office, plus Glasgow) that earned the archive's most consistent supplier endorsement — recommended independently by different members every year from 2021 to 2026.

## Track record (as reported)

- **2021**: adopted as the escape from Hillebrand/Wineflow post-Brexit — "gruff but get the job done", well priced.
- **2022**: "moved everything to them as Hillebrand are hopeless"; the most widely used EU/international forwarder; reliable with temperature-controlled containers. Known limits: no Norway, no small Portugal loads; occasional Far East transshipment delays.
- **2023–24**: repeatedly the pick for Mexico–UK/US (tequila/mezcal lanes) — "no issues", several named account contacts circulated; cheaper France freight than glassmakers' own arrangements.
- **2025–26**: still the "go to" for bond-to-bond moves and US shipping.

## Where members used alternatives

SupplyChain21 for bonded UK pallet moves; JS Wines & Spirits and KC Shipping when Mexico quotes needed beating; Wineflow for Italy/full-trailer EU runs; DHL for parcels/samples. See [[fulfilment-and-3pl]] for the full landscape.
