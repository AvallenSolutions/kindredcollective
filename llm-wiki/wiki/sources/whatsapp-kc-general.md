# WhatsApp Archive — KC General (2025–2026)

**What this is:** the export of "Kindred Collective Main Feed" (renamed "KC General") — ~6,000 messages from 11 March 2025 to 8 June 2026. This is the paid-membership successor to [[whatsapp-kindred-spirits]], created when the free group converted to the £8.99/month Kindred Collective.

## Shape of the archive

- Created 11 March 2025 as the central hub of the paid community (Nas.io platform), with niche topics pushed to category sub-groups (beer, B Corp, EPR, golf). ~210 members migrated by April 2025; a later win-back campaign (3 months free) targeted those who hadn't. Passed 256 members by November 2025.
- Ingested into this wiki 2026-07-13 via 3 anonymised extraction passes (same rules as the original archive).

## Major threads unique to this period

- **The Law Distribution crisis (June 2025)** — the bonded 3PL gave every brand under 500 pallets 30 days' notice; the group ran a collective warehousing tender in response. See [[law-distribution]] and [[fulfilment-and-3pl]].
- **Distributor gatekeeping (February 2026)** — documented cases of large wholesalers refusing to list independents to protect major-supplier relationships, with calls for competition-authority scrutiny. See [[route-to-market]].
- **Diageo's H1 FY2026 stumble** analysed as an opening for independents (organic sales −2.8%, North America tequila −23%). See [[route-to-market]].
- **Feb 2026 duty uprating tracked in real time** — members computed per-bottle impact of the £33.99/£30.62 rates; corroborates the gov.uk capture in [[alcohol-duty]].
- **EPR maturing** — supplier surcharge disputes (Vetroelite £240/tonne), PACKUK billing errors, Fever-Tree's legal action. See [[extended-producer-responsibility]].
- **Platform history** — the kindredcollective.co.uk rebuild (launched ~30 March 2026: Explore/Requests/Offers/Events/News/Community), the February 2026 Annual Awards (~130 capacity, Red Bull Arches), and the launch of member-founded marketplace DrinksBox. All on [[kindred-collective]].
- **US tariffs and the UK–India FTA** — new export-market economics discussed at length ([[uk-drinks-legislation-and-tax]] gaps list).
