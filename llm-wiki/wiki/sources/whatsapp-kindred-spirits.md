# WhatsApp Archive — Kindred Spirits (2018–2025)

**What this is:** the full export of the original "Kindred Spirits" WhatsApp group — ~37,500 messages from 21 May 2018 to 11 April 2025, when the group closed and the community migrated to the paid Kindred Collective (final message: *"The Kind' is dead. Long live the Kind'"*). This is the founding knowledge asset of [[kindred-collective]].

## Shape of the archive

- Founded May 2018 by the founder of The Duppy Share as a ~15-person circle to "share our collective failings so make success quicker to come by". Quiet until April 2021, when a bulk-add took it to ~150 members and it became a daily Q&A firehose.
- Growth: ~200 members (late 2021) → 300 (May 2023) → 400 (Dec 2023) → ~450 with a survey-linked cull (mid 2024) → 550+ at closure. Message volume peaked in 2024 (~13,500 messages).
- Ingested into this wiki 2026-07-13 via 13 chunked, anonymised extraction passes. Member names never cross into wiki pages; company and brand names do.

## What the archive contains (routed to concept pages)

- **Supplier experience en masse** — the single biggest category: glass ([[glass-supply]]), 3PL/bonded warehousing ([[fulfilment-and-3pl]]), canning ([[rtd-canning]]), labels, closures, freight ([[albatrans]]), plus repeated-warning suppliers ([[enotria]], [[scam-warnings]]). Routed via [[supplier-intelligence]].
- **Regulation lived in real time** — the 2021 duty reform announcement through the Aug 2023 implementation ([[alcohol-duty]]), the Scottish DRS collapse ([[deposit-return-scheme]]), EPR arrival ([[extended-producer-responsibility]]), WOWGR→APPA transition and the R&D tax credit crackdown ([[hmrc-compliance]]).
- **Trading benchmarks** — margins, listing fees, rate-of-sale norms, fundraising mechanics ([[finance-and-ops-benchmarks]]) and the distributor landscape ([[route-to-market]]).
- **Community history** — meetups, awards, the CGA insights partnership, group-buying initiatives, and the migration to the paid collective (all on [[kindred-collective]]).

## Period themes at a glance

- **2021**: post-Covid supply crisis (cans, cardboard, glass, HGV drivers); duty reform announced.
- **2022**: glass crisis deepens (Ukraine war), courier breakage wars, insolvency lessons (Farmdrop), Scotland DRS anxiety begins.
- **2023**: DRS collapse; duty reform lands (+10% spirits); Enotria payment saga; first Awards and Christmas party.
- **2024**: leaking-cans saga; R&D credit clawbacks; Diageo cuts wholesalers; EPR looms; Drink It In festival; group professionalises (CGA surveys, quarterly intakes).
- **2025 (to Apr)**: APPA rollout chaos; EPR live; group-buying tenders; conversion to paid membership.
