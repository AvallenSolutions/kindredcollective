# No/Lo Alcohol

**What this is:** the no- and low-alcohol drinks category. This page exists because two otherwise unrelated sources meet here — the PRD treats No/Lo as a first-class brand category, and the duty tables explain why the category is economically attractive.

## From the product side

[[prd-website-rebuild]] lists No/Lo alongside Spirits, Beer, Wine and RTD as one of five brand categories on [[kindred-collective]] — it's a segment the platform expects to filter, showcase and match to suppliers, not a niche tag.

## From the tax side

[[gov-uk-alcohol-duty-rates]] shows the structural advantage:

- **0–1.2% ABV: £0.00 duty** — alcohol-free and most "0.5%" products pay no excise duty at all.
- **1.3–3.4% ABV: £9.96/litre of pure alcohol** — the "low" band, less than half the beer standard rate (£22.58) and under 40% of the wine/spirits rate (£26.61) in the next band up.

So the [[alcohol-duty]] regime deliberately rewards exactly the products this category makes — a cost advantage that compounds with excise-free export/retail handling.
