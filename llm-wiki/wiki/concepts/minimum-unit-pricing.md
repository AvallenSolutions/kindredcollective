# Minimum Unit Pricing (MUP)

**What this is:** legal floor prices for alcohol retail in parts of the UK — Scotland at **65p per unit** (since 2024; 50p from 2018) and Wales at 50p per unit (since March 2020). England and Northern Ireland have no MUP. Full Scottish detail in [[gov-scot-minimum-unit-pricing]].

## The mechanics

Floor price = rate × units (units = ABV × litres × 100). At 65p: a 700ml spirit at 40% can never retail in Scotland below **£18.20**; a 750ml wine at 13% never below £6.34. It's not a tax — no duty interaction, and the retained margin sits with the retailer, not the producer or Treasury.

## What it means for Kindred members

- **Promotions hit the floor first.** Deep discounting, meal deals and multi-buy pricing into Scottish grocery/convenience must be checked against the floor — a "25% off" promo on a £20 RRP bottle of 40% spirit breaks the law in Scotland (£15 < £18.20). Wales at 50p bites lower (£14 floor for the same bottle) but still catches aggressive promotions.
- **Premium spirits are barely touched** — most member products already retail above their floor. The regime mainly reshapes the value end, which slightly flatters premium positioning against cheap own-label.
- **Low-ABV products get low floors**: a 5% ABV 250ml RTD carries a floor of just 81p in Scotland — MUP is no obstacle to the [[no-lo-alcohol]] and RTD categories, compounding their duty advantage ([[alcohol-duty]]).
- **Divergence is the operational cost**: three different pricing regimes across GB (65p / 50p / none) mean per-nation price files — the same devolution pattern as the [[deposit-return-scheme]].
- The community saw the 2024 rise coming: press reports of a ~30% increase circulated in the group in February 2024 ([[whatsapp-kindred-spirits]]), matching the confirmed 50p→65p change.

## Maintenance note

Scotland's rate is periodically reviewed (the 2024 vote continued a policy that had a sunset provision); check the rate after Scottish budget events. Wales's 50p rate and any England/NI adoption are worth a lint-pass check.
